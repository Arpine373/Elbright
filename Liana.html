About Us

Hi, I am Liana the founder of LBright educational center.
I have created LBright educational center in 2022 and in just one year became very successful in Armenia.
LBright cooperates with different Universities, colleges, schools around the world.
We offer various types of programs and also support in the process of it.
Our students will get opportunities which will satisfy their requirements according to academic standards.

Here are our goals

  • Be a connection between you and international world education
  • Contribute to the Achievement of your educational goals
  • Support find the right program for you and apply without barriers
  • Assist you gain a quality education beyond the border

    • Trust LBRIGHT, make your future BRIGHT.
